Salient Features

  • GPSB Eco Series Machine – The two-stage process 3 Cavity Stretch Blow Moulding Machine with simple & compact design for Beverage & other applications.
  • Telemecanique Schneider PLC & HMI – Controlled by high-performance Schneider Electric Modicon M200 PLC with 4MB SD card memory and rugged construction operated by HMI with TFT 7” colour touch screen, supporting more than 2000 recipes and Ethernet / USB ports for connectivity.
  • 40 Ton Toggle Mould Clamping System ensures less than hair parting line in the bottles.
  • High Pressure Diaphragm Type Blowing Valve – Highly Efficient High Pressure Diaphragm Type Parker Make Blowing Valve with No Preform No Blow System for reduced Air consumption.
  • Direct Preform Transfer Mechanism from Infrared Pre-Heating unit to Blowing unit for reduced Manpower & achieve Maximum Production Output.
  • Automatic Bottle Ejection with Gripper Mechanism for reduced Manpower.
  • Revolving Infrared Preform heating with Air Circulating System – 1900 MM Two Module 7 Zone Infrared Heating Length with Conveyorized Preheating System to ensures uniform heating of thick wall preforms with less power consumption.
  • Preform Pre-heating Conveyor Electrical Type controlled with AC Variable Frequency drive – Bonfiglioli Gear Motor with AC Variable Drive.
